We use Blue Violet and Vivid Sky Blue as important as main colours which are for buttons and icons. These vibrant hues not only catch the eye but also convey a sense of dynamism and clarity, ensuring a seamless and engaging user experience.

Colours Contrast Checker

All our colors have passed a 5.0 rating for color contrast checking. In the gradient color mixes, we've selected combinations like Blue Violet & Rose (#F94E9B + #910CEA) and Chrysler Blue & Vivid Sky Blue (#4A10D1 + #4CC9F0). We've ensured that the darker colors, such as Blue Violet and Chrysler Blue (which have passed the contrast checker), dominate the mix, providing ample space while still maintaining strong visual contrast.

Typography

Roboto

Roboto is a geometric sans-serif typeface family developed by Google and available for free on the web.

Roboto is a popular choice for app design because it has a friendly and open curve. This makes the reading rhythm more natural. In 2024, Roboto is one of 30 best modern typeface for web and app design.

Roboto is a highly versatile typeface that works well across various screen sizes and resolutions. It was specifically designed by Google to be readable and clear on both small mobile screens and larger desktop displays.

LOSTAR

Lostar boasts distinctive and bold letterforms, making it an ideal choice for modern and minimalist design themes. Its unique ligatures on serif offer a cool and standout vibe compared to regular serif fonts, making it particularly suited for logos that evoke a dreamy atmosphere.
