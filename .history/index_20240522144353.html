Secondary Colours

We use Blue Violet and Vivid Sky Blue as important as main colours which are for buttons and icons. These vibrant hues not only catch the eye but also convey a sense of dynamism and clarity, ensuring a seamless and engaging user experience.

Additionally, we pick a specific gray shade for the navigation bar, backgrounds of cards, and separators. This helps to organize the app neatly and makes it easier for users to find their way around.
